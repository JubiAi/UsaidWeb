KHUSHI LIVE Privacy Policy

WHAT IS KHUSHI LIVE?

Khushi Live is a unique chatbot, designed to provide the youth between 18-24 years with much-needed guidance on the topic of sexual health awareness.

WHO MAINTAINS KHUSHI?

Khushi Live has been launched by Jubi.ai in collaboration with ABT - SHOPS PLUS an initiative of USAID. Jubi.ai is a Mumbai based chatbot agency that seeks social impact. You can contact us here - Jubi.ai, GC-2, Gundecha Onclave, Sakinaka, Andheri East, Mumbai 400058
If you have any questions or concerns relating to our privacy policy or how we capture, store or use your personal information, please feel free to contact us at srh@jubi.ai.

WHAT INFORMATION DOES KHUSHI SEEK?

As part of our policy, we are required to maintain a log of the information you provide when you interact with Khushi. This includes your email / social media handle, gender, location and name, and any information you share with us in the course of using our service, including medical information that you share.

HOW DO WE USE THIS INFORMATION?

We will enrich our database to provide you with the best information on health, manage referrals, and solicit feedback on your experience with Khushi and its partners. With our partners Abt and USAID, we may also use some kinds of information for research and analytics purposes. Any published research results will involve only anonymized or aggregated information. No personal information of the users will be shared with third parties.

COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS AND LAW ENFORCEMENT

Khushi cooperates with government and law enforcement officials and private parties to enforce and comply with the law. We will disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials or private parties as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to respond to claims and legal process (including but not limited to subpoenas), to protect the property and rights of Khushi or a third party, to protect the safety of the public or any person, or to prevent or stop any illegal, unethical or legally actionable activity.
