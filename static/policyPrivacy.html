Privacy Policy

What is HelloJubi?

HelloJubi is a unique chatbot, designed to provide the youth between 18-29 years with much-needed guidance on the topic of sexual health awareness.

Who maintains HelloJubi?

HelloJubi has been launched by Jubi.ai in collaboration with ABT - SHOPS PLUS an initiative of USAID. Jubi.AI is a Mumbai based chatbot agency that seeks social impact. You can contact us here - Jubi.ai, GC-2, Gundecha Onclave, Sakinaka, Andheri East, Mumbai 400058

If you have any questions or concerns relating to our privacy policy or how we capture, store or use your personal information, please feel free to contact us at srh@jubi.ai.

What information does HelloJubi seek?

As part of our policy, we are required to maintain a log of the information you provide when you interact with HelloJubi. This includes your email / social media handles, gender, location and name, and any information you share with us in the course of using our service, including medical information that you share.

How do we use this information?

We will enrich our database to provide you with the best information on health, manage referrals, and solicit feedback on your experience with HelloJubii and its partners. With our partners Abt and USAID, we may also use some kinds of information for research and analytics purposes. Any published research results will involve only anonymized or aggregated information. No personal information of the users will be shared with third parties.

Compliance with laws and law enforcement

HelloJubi cooperates with government and law enforcement officials and private parties to enforce and comply with the law. We will disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials or private parties as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to respond to claims and legal process (including but not limited to subpoenas), to protect the property and rights of HelloJubi or a third party, to protect the safety of the public or any person, or to prevent or stop any illegal, unethical or legally actionable activity.

User's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 15 years since the chatbot is designed to speak with those who are above this age.

To ensure the privacy of our users, we use a variety of security measures, including sophisticated encryption and authentication tools. Our personal information is protected using secure networks and accessible only to authorised personnel who are contractually bound to keep the information confidential. Your privacy matters and we are committed to honour the integrity and security of your data.

Sharing information

We will never provide your information to third parties, agencies or companies for marketing or commercial purposes. We will not share your personal information without your explicit consent unless required to do so upon governmental request, in response to a court order, when required by law enforcement agencies, to enforce our website policies, or to protect our or others' rights, property, or safety.
